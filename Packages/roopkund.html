THE MAGNIFICENT ROOPKUND

THE MAGNIFICENT ROOPKUND

08 Days 07 Nights
Best Season Summer (May, June, July) Monsoon (Aug, Sept, Oct)

Who should attend?

One of the well-known treks in India's Greater Himalaya is the Roopkund Trek. Roopkund Trek is incredibly exciting due to more than just the snow and high altitude. The main draw of this walk is a lake that is surrounded by a large number of human skeletons. Trekkers from all over the world travel to this location to observe this enigmatic lake. Roopkund is a very high altitude trek. This trek demands you to trek for 8 days so you need to have proper physical fitness, lots of stamina and endurance.
