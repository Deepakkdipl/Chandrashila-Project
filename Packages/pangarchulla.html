The Challenging-Pangarchulla Trek

The Challenging-Pangarchulla Trek

07 Days 06 Nights
Best Season March April May September October November
A high-altitude hike known as the Pangarchulla Peak is located in the Joshimath area of the Chamoli district in Uttarakhand. This moderately difficult trip will take you through enormous boulders, tall peaks, expansive paths, and stunning deodar trees. Situated at a height of 15069 feet lies the Pangarchulla Peak. Another name for the Pangarchulla peak trip is the Pangarchulla trek summit.

For those who like mountains, trekking, and adventure, this trek is ideal. Owing to its elevated location, the Pangarchulla journey is highly challenging and requires six to seven days. The oxygen level is likewise low because of its high altitude.

The Pangarchulla Peak Trek begins at Joshimath and travels across Kuari Pass. You will pass vast boulders (stone), tall peaks, extensive paths, and breathtaking Deodar woods on your route to Pangarchulla in the middle.

In the midst of a deep forest, you might be fortunate enough to spot musk deer, bears, Himalayan leopards, and a variety of other plants. You will walk in deep snow during the Pangarchulla Peak Trek, which passes through stunning meadows (Bugyal) and offers an absolutely breathtaking perspective of the Himalayas. Nature enthusiasts would like this hike. The walk path passes through an area of verdant foliage before entering a winter wonderland.
